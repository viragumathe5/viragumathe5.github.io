I started out getting machines to understand human language. Then I thought — why not also make them sprint? These days I bounce between Natural Language Processing and High-Performance Computing, where I try to make algorithms smarter and simulations faster (sometimes at the same time). I also spend a suspicious amount of time talking to myself while debugging.

Currently I am a master's student in High Performance Computing at Edinburgh Parallel Computing Center. Along with my master's I am also working on knowledge extraction of the 18th century english newspaper called "The Spiritualist", The project is originally governed by National Library of Scotland and Encyclopedia Britannica.

Apart from tech I play badminton and recently started squash. I often try to see the world from my lense. I like astrophotography.

Currently I am looking for the full time roles in UK and Europe in NLP or Quantitative Trading/Research.
